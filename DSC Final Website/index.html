Our Motto

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups where students grow their knowledge in a peer to peer learning environment.

Developer Student Club NUV is a Google recognized student community at Navrachana University which is directly connected with the tech giant Google. DSC NUV conducts workshops, seminars and other fun learning activities which help the students increase their skills.

Connect

At DSC NUV, our aim is to teach and learn. Developers come together under one roof to share knowledge.

Warm Community

An excellent community of people engaged in every field which are always ready to help and share the knowledge.

Professionals

Our community also have professionals who have a lot of experience in the corporate sector which helps the community.

Team

Our team consists of students as well as professionals which makes our community powerful.

FAQ's

What is DSC?

Developer Students' Club is a community powered by google for university students. It helps the students to develop skills in various domains.

What is Our Aim?

DSC NUV is a community which directly reports to Google Developers for the workshops, seminars and other fun learning activities conducted within the community.

How can i be a part?

Just take part in our events and have networking session and you are a part of DSC NUV.

Who can join?

Students from any field or domain are welcomed to join our community and help in the welfare of the community.

When should i apply?

You can apply anytime to be the part of our community and help in increasing the community.

How can I get updates?

Just visit the website regularly for the updates. For any queries drop a mail of your query to the mentioned e-mail.
